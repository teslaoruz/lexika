# Lexika — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 18 June 2026_

This policy explains what data Lexika collects, why, and your choices. Lexika is
an English-vocabulary learning app for students.

## 1. Data we collect
**You provide:**
- Account: email address, password (stored only as a salted hash — never in
  plaintext), display name (optional), and native language preference.

**Created as you use Lexika:**
- Learning data: words you look up, decks, review results, SM-2 scheduling
  state, XP, streaks, and per-answer review history.
- Class data: the class (cohort) you create or join, and your weekly XP shown on
  that class's leaderboard.

We do **not** intentionally collect special-category data, payment data, or
precise location.

## 2. How we use it
- To provide the core features: lookups, spaced-repetition review, games,
  progress, suggestions, and class leaderboards.
- To keep you signed in (a session token stored on your device).
- To maintain and debug the service.

We do **not** sell your personal data or use it for third-party advertising.

## 3. Third-party services
To provide content, word and translation data may be sent to external services
when a word is first looked up (results are then cached so repeat lookups stay
local):
- **Free Dictionary API** (`api.dictionaryapi.dev`) — English definitions,
  phonetics, examples, audio links.
- **Google Translate** (via `deep-translator`) — machine translations into your
  native language.

Only the word being looked up is sent — not your identity or account data. These
providers have their own privacy policies.

## 4. What other users can see
Members of a class you join can see your **display name** and **weekly XP** on
the leaderboard. Nothing else (your email, individual reviews, or other classes)
is shared with other users.

## 5. Data retention and deletion
We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your
account and associated personal data at any time (see §8); cached, anonymized
dictionary content (not tied to you) may be retained to serve other learners.

## 6. Security
Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2 and a per-user salt. Access to your data
requires your session token. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly
secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data. Always use Lexika
over a secure (HTTPS) connection in production.

## 7. Children
Lexika is used in classroom settings and may be used by minors under a teacher's
or guardian's supervision. We collect only the minimal data needed to provide
the learning service. Parents, guardians, or teachers may request access to or
deletion of a student's data (see §8).

## 8. Your rights and contact
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export,
or delete your data. To exercise them, or for any privacy question, contact:
**irshad.oruzgani@gmail.com**.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be communicated in-app or by
email.
